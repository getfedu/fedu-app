About FEDU



What's fedu?

fedu is a further education webapp. It's for Developers and Designers, who want to learn something new.
But fedu dont provide tutorial videos, there are plenty of others for this purpouse.
Our goal is to provide a webapp where people can watch e.g. in groups and discuss the subject later.
Just like your visiting a conference, but without the traveling.



The Idea

The idea for fedu was born by us, three students of Interactive Media in Augbsurg, Germany.
Two of us (Martin and Mathias), started fedu as their Bachelor-Thesis in November 2012.
Many long weeks of programming and discussing and more programming, we launched fedu in July 2013.

Sorry, for all non-german-speaking people, But we're not that good in the english language to make such a video :(
